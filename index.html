Vision

The vision is to eliminate programming languages as barriers by pioneering a machine-to-machine natural language computing paradigm, enabling seamless, code-free communication between computational systems. This research aims to fundamentally transform human-machine interaction and redefine the future of computing by making natural language the universal interface for all computational processes.

Research

The research methodology consists of iterative refinement using a multi-agent research assistant for autonomous knowledge synthesis, hardware-software co-design for optimized computation, and systematic exploration of post-programming natural language interfaces. Our approach combines systems-level thinking with breakthrough AI research to eliminate coding as an intermediary layer.
